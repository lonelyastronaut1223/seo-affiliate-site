Travel packing tips

Keep it light

One body, one zoom, one prime. Add a mini tripod and 2 spare batteries.

Backup footage daily

Carry dual cards or a tiny SSD. Offload each night to avoid lost clips.

Weather prep

Pack a rain cover and microfiber cloth; humidity and dust happen fast on trips.

Panasonic Lumix S5II

Panasonic finally fixed their autofocus. The S5II uses Phase Detect AF (PDAF), making it reliable for solo shooters. But its superpower is "Open Gate" 6K video, which lets you record the full sensor height.

Why does that matter? It allows you to crop a vertical 9:16 (TikTok) and a horizontal 16:9 (YouTube) video from the same clip without losing quality. It also has a built-in fan for unlimited recording.

Hybrid Buying Advice

The Lens Ecosystem

If you choose Sony, you get cheap third-party lenses. If you choose Canon, you get expensive but brilliant proprietary lenses. Choose the system, not just the body.

Do you need 33MP?

24MP is plenty for 4K video and social media. Only pay extra for the Sony A7 IV's 33MP if you crop heavily or print huge billboards.

Key differences

Autofocus

Both excellent; Canon is stickier for humans/animals. Sony strong across varied subjects and lenses.

Rolling shutter

A7 V improves over A7 IV but R6 III remains cleaner for fast motion and pans.

Stabilisierung

R6 III IBIS better for handheld video; Sony Active helps but crops.

Heat

Both improved; Canon still reliable for longer 4K sessions. Sony better than older A7 IV.

Lenses

Sony: massive third-party and native options. Canon RF: great glass but pricier, fewer third-party AF choices.

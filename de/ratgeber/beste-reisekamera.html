Travel Tips

One Lens Rule

Try to Reise with just one versatile zoom (like a 24-70mm equivalent). Changing lenses in a dusty market or windy beach is a recipe for dirty sensors.

Back up Daily

SD cards fail. Bring a small SSD or use Wi-Fi to transfer your best shots to your phone every evening.

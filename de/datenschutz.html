cameraupick ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Short version: We don't sell your data. We use standard analytics to improve the site and affiliate links to earn commissions. That's it.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our site, we automatically collect:

  • Analytics data: Page views, time on site, device type, browser, referring source (via Google Analytics or similar)
  • Cookies: Small text files that help us remember your preferences and measure site performance
  • Log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited

Information You Provide

If you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us, we collect:

  • Email address: For newsletter delivery or responding to inquiries
  • Name (optional): If you choose to provide it
  • Message content: Any information you include in emails or contact forms

How We Use Your Data

Site Improvement

We analyze traffic patterns to understand what content is useful, which pages need improvement, and where users struggle with navigation.

Communication

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we'll send you new reviews, buying guides, and deals. You can unsubscribe anytime with one click.

Affiliate Tracking

When you click an affiliate link, cookies track your purchase so we can earn a commission. This doesn't identify you personally and doesn't increase your price.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic. Google may use cookies and collect data about your browsing. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Amazon Associates

We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click our Amazon links, Amazon sets cookies to track purchases for commission purposes.

Email Service Provider

If we offer a newsletter, we use a third-party provider (like Mailchimp or ConvertKit) to manage subscriptions. Your email is stored securely according to their privacy policies.

Social Media Platforms

Our social media buttons may allow those platforms (YouTube, Twitter, Instagram) to track your visit. We have no control over their data practicesâ€”please review their privacy policies.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential functionality: Remembering your preferences (dark mode, language settings)
  • Analytics: Measuring site performance and user behavior
  • Affiliate tracking: Crediting us for referred purchases

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit site functionality.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • Limited access to any collected personal information
  • Regular security updates

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Access & Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (via browser settings or opt-out tools)

Kontakt us at cameraupick@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

EU & UK Users (GDPR)

If you're in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under GDPR, including:

  • Right to rectification (correct inaccurate data)
  • Right to data portability (transfer your data elsewhere)
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

California Users (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, delete personal information, and opt out of sale (note: we don't sell personal information).

Children's Datenschutz

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately and we'll delete it.

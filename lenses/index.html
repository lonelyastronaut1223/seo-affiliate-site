Understanding Lens Basics

üìê Focal Length

Wide (16-35mm): Landscapes, architecture, vlogging
Standard (35-50mm): Everyday, street photography
Portrait (85-135mm): Beautiful background blur
Telephoto (70-200mm+): Sports, wildlife

üîÜ Aperture (f-number)

f/1.4-f/2: Amazing low light, creamy bokeh, expensive
f/2.8: Professional standard, good balance
f/4: Lighter, cheaper, still great for most uses
f/5.6+: Variable zooms, budget friendly

‚ö° Prime vs Zoom

Prime lenses: Fixed focal length, sharper, faster apertures, lighter, cheaper
Zoom lenses: Versatile, one lens for many situations, heavier, more expensive for fast apertures

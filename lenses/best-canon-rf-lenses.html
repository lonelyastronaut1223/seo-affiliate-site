Canon Lens Ecosystem Notes

RF vs RF-S

RF lenses: Full-frame coverage, work on all Canon mirrorless cameras.
RF-S lenses: APS-C only (R50, R10, R7), more compact and affordable.

Third-Party Options

Currently limited. Viltrox makes affordable AF primes. Sigma/Tamron are working on RF lenses. For now, Canon native is your best bet.

EF Lens Adapters

Canon's EF-RF adapters work flawlessly with old EF/EF-S DSLR lenses. Great way to save money using used EF glass.

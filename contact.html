Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept review units from manufacturers?

No. We purchase or rent all cameras ourselves to maintain complete independence. This ensures our reviews are unbiased and honest.

Can I sponsor a review or get my product featured?

No. We do not accept paid placements, sponsored content, or any form of compensation that would influence our editorial decisions. Our top picks are based solely on merit.

How do you make money?

We use affiliate links (primarily Amazon Associates). When you purchase through our links, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This keeps the site ad-free while maintaining editorial independence.

Can you review a specific camera?

We appreciate suggestions! Email us at cameraupick@gmail.com with your request. We prioritize cameras based on reader interest and availability, but we can't guarantee coverage of every request.

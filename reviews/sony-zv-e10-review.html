Sony ZV-E10 Review

Entry-level vlog camera with great autofocus and creator-friendly tools.

Pros

Autofocus

Excellent face/eye tracking and Product Showcase mode.

Creator features

Flip screen, tally lamp, mic jack, digital audio through hotshoe.

Value

Low price leaves budget for lenses and audio.

Cons

No 4K60

Capped at 4K30; 1080p slow motion available.

No EVF

LCD-only shooting in bright sun is tougher.

Rolling shutter

Fast pans can wobble; keep camera stable.

Who should buy it

Beginners and travel vloggers who want strong autofocus and a small setup. If you need 4K60 or an EVF, step up to the A6700.

Recommended add-ons

Wide lens

Sony 10-20mm PZ for handheld vlogs without heavy crop.

Mic

Small on-camera shotgun or wireless lav to fix windy audio.

Support

Mini tripod/grip with Bluetooth makes framing and run-and-gun easier.

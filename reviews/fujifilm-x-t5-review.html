Fujifilm X-T5 Review

High-resolution APS-C with classic dials and excellent photo quality.

Pros

Image quality

40MP detail, great dynamic range, lovely color science.

Handling

Dedicated dials, lightweight body, weather-sealed.

IBIS

In-body stabilization helps handheld stills and video.

Cons

Rolling shutter

Not ideal for fast video pans in 6K/4K HQ.

AF vs Sony/Canon

Improved, but tracking still trails the best for video.

Flip vs tilt

Three-way tilt is great for photo, less ideal for selfie video.

Who should buy it

Photo-first shooters who value color and classic controls. If you prioritize heavy video work, consider X-S20 or a Sony/Canon hybrid instead.

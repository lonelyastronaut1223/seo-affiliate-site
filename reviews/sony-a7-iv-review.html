Sony A7 IV Review

Balanced full-frame hybrid: great autofocus, color, and lens ecosystem.

Pros

Autofocus

Fast, sticky eye AF for humans and animals; reliable in video.

10-bit video

S-Log3 and S-Cinetone with 4K30 oversampled from 7K.

E-mount lenses

Huge lens ecosystem across price points.

Cons

Rolling shutter

Not ideal for fast pans in 4K24/30.

Menus

Better than before, still dense for newcomers.

Price

Body plus good lenses adds up quickly.

Who should buy it

Hybrid shooters who want one body for weddings, travel, and YouTube. If you prioritize fast sports video or need no rolling shutter, consider an A7S III/FX3 instead.
